Digital Business Card (NameCard)

A fully digital business card that lives behind a QR code or short link. Centralise your contact details, social profiles and key links in one place, then update everything without reprinting any physical cards.

  • Share one QR code or link instead of stacks of paper cards.
  • Update your profile once and keep all recipients up to date.
  • Add photo, role, contact channels and key URLs on a single page.

Open NameCard

Befitting – Smart Fitting Experience

Give your customers a virtual fitting room in their pocket. By scanning the QR code on each garment, Befitting compares the item with their saved body profile and instantly suggests the most suitable size.

  • Link each garment to a QR code for instant size advice.
  • Use body profiles and measurements instead of guesswork.
  • Reduce fitting time and returns for in-store or online shopping.

Concept app in development – demo available on request.

TaskTicket – Light Project Tracker

A simple ticket view for following marketing and design requests. Group tasks by status, keep briefs and comments attached to each item, and maintain a clear overview of what is in progress or completed.

  • Capture marketing and design tasks as clear, trackable tickets.
  • Organise work by status so priorities are visible at a glance.
  • Keep briefs, notes and decisions attached to each ticket.

Internal tool used at CDC – client-ready version planned.

Web Creator – Newsletter & Page Builder

A visual editor for building newsletters and simple presentation pages with a fixed header and footer. Use branded content blocks to assemble layouts without writing code.

  • Compose newsletters and pages from reusable content sections.
  • Keep a consistent header, footer and brand style across all layouts.
  • Export clean HTML or PDF that is ready to send or print.

Currently used in CDC projects – can be adapted to your brand needs.
